School Video

Cafeteria

The cafeteria is a place of many memories, you can meet friends that are not in your class. You can fill your tummy and talk with the teachers who come around.

Park

The park is place to play with your friends and slide down the slides together. You can climb the monkey bars or do the hula hoops. There are an endless number of things you can choose to do! Sometimes in spring you may search for ladybugs, while in winter you would probably do a running race to keep yourself warm.

Library

The school library is a great place to learn and research. The school librarian can help you find books and assist you on walk around the library. There are many cozy spots to read around the library like on the beanbags. If you have a research project for school, you can find a seat in the library.
